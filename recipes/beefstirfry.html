Beef Stir Fry

Description

Quick and easy beef stir-fry. I make this on my busiest weeknights.

Need to use up some of your leftover vegetables and other pantry staple ingredients? Look no further than a classic beef stir-fry. This beef and broccoli stir-fry comes together in just 25 minutes and only requires ingredients that you probably already have.

Ingredients

  • Oil: This traditional recipe starts with olive oil.
  • Guanciale: Buy guanciale at your local butcher. You can always use bacon or pancetta.
  • Spaghetti: Use store-bought or homemade spaghetti noodles.
  • Eggs: Three large eggs add richness and flavor.
  • Cheese: Hand shredded Pecorino cheese. You can substitute Parmesan, if preferred.
  • Seasonings: This recipe is best seasoned simply with salt and pepper.

Steps:

  1. Cook the pork in olive oil until browned and crispy, then drain on paper towels.

  2. Boil the spaghetti in salted water. Drain and return to the pot. Let cool.

  3. Whisk the eggs, 1/2 of the cheese, and some pepper in a bowl until smooth.

  4. Pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring quickly, until creamy.

  5. Stir in the pork, then top with the remaining cheese and more black pepper.
